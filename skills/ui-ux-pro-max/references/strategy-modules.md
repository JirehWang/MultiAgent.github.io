# UI UX Pro Max Strategy Modules

This reference exists to keep `ui-ux-pro-max` lightweight. Use it to remember what to extract, not as a full style catalog.

## industry-fit

Identify the product category, audience, credibility needs, emotional tone, CTA type, and industry-specific trust cues. Examples: medical needs calm + credibility; finance needs restraint + trust; church/nonprofit needs warmth + clarity; SaaS needs product clarity + conversion.

## layout-pattern

Pick one dominant pattern: hero-centric, trust-authority, social-proof-focused, storytelling-driven, feature-rich showcase, conversion-optimized, editorial grid, dashboard-dense, or product-demo-led.

## palette-type

Pick 1-2 directions: warm neutral, civic trust, editorial monochrome, premium dark, soft wellness, technical blue, sustainable green, high-contrast creative, luxury muted, or playful bright. Do not enumerate full palettes unless needed.

## typography-pairing

Pick 1-2 moods: readable sans + gentle serif, modern grotesk + mono accent, editorial serif + neutral sans, geometric sans + humanist sans, or enterprise sans + code mono.

## ux-checklist

Select only task-relevant checks: CTA visibility, section rhythm, responsive breakpoints, tap targets, form states, keyboard focus, contrast, reduced motion, loading/empty/error states, navigation clarity.

## anti-patterns

Name the specific bad defaults for this task: AI-purple gradients, generic SaaS card grids, glassmorphism everywhere, weak headline scale, low contrast, ungrounded motion, overplayful healthcare/finance, startup look for community/nonprofit, copied reference-site assets.
