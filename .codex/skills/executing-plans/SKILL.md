# Executing Plans

Use this skill when the user gives or approves a plan and wants it implemented.

## Workflow

1. Read the whole plan before editing.
2. Check for blockers, missing prerequisites, unsafe steps, or vague verification.
3. If the plan is executable, create a working checklist.
4. Execute steps in dependency order.
5. Keep edits scoped to the plan.
6. Update progress as steps complete.
7. Verify the final integrated result, not only individual steps.
8. Report deviations, blockers, and evidence.

## Rules

- Do not silently change the plan's intent.
- Do not skip review checkpoints.
- Do not continue past a blocker by inventing requirements.
- If a step is obsolete, explain why and choose the safest equivalent.
- Verification evidence belongs at the end.

## Caveman Compression

For progress notes, use caveman style: step, status, evidence, blocker, next. Do not compress final user-facing explanation when tradeoffs matter.
